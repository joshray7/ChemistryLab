Virtual Laboratory

How the Virtual Lab Works

Learn chemistry experiments in a safe, interactive digital environment — no physical risks, no equipment needed.

🎯

Purpose of the Virtual Lab

The virtual lab is designed to help students understand chemical reactions, laboratory procedures, and scientific observations before performing real experiments. It bridges the gap between theory and practice in a completely safe environment.

📋

How to Use the Virtual Lab

01

Select an experiment from the available list on the platform.

02

Follow the on-screen instructions carefully, step by step.

03

Observe the reactions, color changes, and other results as they happen.

04

Record your results and write your conclusions based on observations.

✨

Advantages of Virtual Labs

No Safety RisksExperiment freely without exposure to hazardous chemicals or equipment.

ReusableRepeat any experiment as many times as needed at no cost.

Easier UnderstandingVisual simulations make complex reactions easier to grasp.

Accessible AnytimeLearn at your own pace, anywhere, on any device.
