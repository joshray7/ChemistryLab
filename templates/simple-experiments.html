Simple Chemistry Experiments

Simple chemistry experiments help students understand chemical principles through observation and practice.

🧪 Experiment 1: Acid and Base Reaction

Mixing an acid with a base produces a neutral substance called salt and water. This process is known as neutralization.

🌡️ Experiment 2: Changes of State

Heating a solid can change it into a liquid, while cooling a liquid can change it into a solid.

💨 Experiment 3: Gas Production

Some chemical reactions produce gases that can be observed through bubbles or pressure changes.

These experiments should be performed virtually or under proper supervision in a real laboratory.
