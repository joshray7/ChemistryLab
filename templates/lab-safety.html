Introduction & Basics

Laboratory Safety Rules

Following safety rules helps prevent accidents, injuries, and damage to equipment. Safety is not optional — it is essential.

⚠️

Always read and understand all safety rules before entering a chemistry laboratory. Your safety and the safety of others depends on it.

📋

General Safety Rules

  • Always follow instructions given by your teacher or lab guide
  • Never eat or drink in the laboratory
  • Wear protective equipment such as lab coats and safety goggles
  • Keep your work area clean and organized
🧪

Handling Chemicals

Chemicals should be handled with care. Never taste chemicals, and avoid inhaling fumes. Always read labels before using any chemical substance and use the minimum amount needed.

🛡️

Accident Prevention

  • Report spills or breakages immediately
  • Do not run or play in the laboratory
  • Wash your hands after experiments
💻

Virtual Laboratory Safety

Although ChemistryLab provides a virtual laboratory, students should understand real laboratory safety rules before performing experiments in real-life labs. Virtual practice builds good habits.

Learning safety rules helps students become responsible scientists and ensures a safe learning environment.
