Laboratory Safety

Following safety rules helps prevent accidents, injuries, and damage to equipment in the chemistry lab.

⚠️

General Safety Rules

  • Always follow instructions given by your teacher or lab guide.
  • Never eat or drink in the laboratory.
  • Wear protective equipment such as lab coats and safety goggles.
  • Keep your work area clean and organized.
🧪

Handling Chemicals

Chemicals should be handled with care. Never taste chemicals, and avoid inhaling fumes. Always read labels before using any chemical substance.

🛡️

Accident Prevention

  • Report spills or breakages immediately.
  • Do not run or play in the laboratory.
  • Wash your hands thoroughly after experiments.
💻

Virtual Laboratory Safety

Although ChemistryLab provides a virtual laboratory, students should understand real laboratory safety rules before performing experiments in real-life labs.

Learning safety rules helps students become responsible scientists and ensures a safe learning environment.
