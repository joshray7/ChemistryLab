SS1 Chemistry

Chemistry is the branch of science that deals with the study of matter, its composition, structure, properties, and the changes it undergoes. In SS1, students are introduced to the basic concepts of chemistry and laboratory practices.

🔬 Introduction to Chemistry

Chemistry helps us understand the materials around us such as air, water, food, medicine, and fuels. It plays an important role in agriculture, medicine, industry, and environmental protection.

🌿 Branches of Chemistry
  • Organic Chemistry – Study of carbon compounds
  • Inorganic Chemistry – Study of non-carbon compounds
  • Physical Chemistry – Study of energy and reactions
  • Analytical Chemistry – Identification of substances
PropertyParams

Matter has various properties that help us identify and classify it. These properties include physical properties (e.g., color, density, melting point) and chemical properties (e.g., reactivity, flammability).

🧊 States of Matter

Matter exists in three main states: solid, liquid, and gas. Solids have fixed shape and volume, liquids have fixed volume but no fixed shape, while gases have neither fixed shape nor volume.

⚠️ Laboratory Safety Rules

Safety in the chemistry laboratory is very important. Students must follow safety rules to prevent accidents.

  • Always wear protective clothing
  • Do not taste chemicals
  • Report spills immediately
  • Follow teacher's instructions
Conclusion

SS1 Chemistry builds the foundation for understanding chemical substances and safe laboratory practices. Mastering these basics is essential for higher classes.
