Organic Chemistry

Organic chemistry deals with carbon compounds such as hydrocarbons, alcohols, and acids. These compounds are important in fuels, plastics, and medicines.

⚡

Electrolysis

Electrolysis is the chemical decomposition of a compound using electricity. It is used in electroplating and extraction of metals.

⏱️

Rates of Chemical Reactions

The rate of a chemical reaction depends on factors such as temperature, concentration, surface area, and catalysts.

🌍

Environmental Chemistry

This aspect of chemistry studies pollution, water treatment, air quality, and ways to protect the environment.

📖

Revision and Exam Preparation

Students should practice past questions, understand practical experiments, and revise key concepts regularly to succeed in examinations.
