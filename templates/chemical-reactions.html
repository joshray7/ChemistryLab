Chemistry Concepts

Chemical Reactions

A chemical reaction changes one or more substances into something new — because atoms rearrange to form new compounds.

⚗️

Reactants and Products

The substances that take part in a chemical reaction are called reactants. The new substances formed after the reaction are called products.

🔬

Types of Chemical Reactions

Combination

Two or more substances combine to form one product.

Decomposition

One substance breaks down into simpler substances.

Displacement

A more reactive element replaces a less reactive one.

Neutralization

An acid reacts with a base to form salt and water.

👁️

Signs of a Chemical Reaction

  • Change in color
  • Formation of gas
  • Temperature change
  • Formation of a precipitate
🧪

Example Reactions

Zinc + HClZn + 2HCl → ZnCl₂ + H₂
Photosynthesis6CO₂ + 6H₂O + light → C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂

Safety Note

🛡️

Always conduct chemical reactions in a controlled environment such as a laboratory, and follow proper safety protocols to prevent accidents and injuries.
